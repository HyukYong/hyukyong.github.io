Controller: Qemica Lab, Republic of Korea [REGISTERED ADDRESS — to be

Contact: feedback.qemica@gmail.com  ·  Data protection contact: same

1. SCOPE
   This policy covers the Qemica desktop application and its optional
   online features. Qemica is local-first: your projects, molecules,
   and results live in files on YOUR computer. Data leaves your machine
   only through the online features below and any third-party services
   you connect yourself (EULA Section 9: IBM Quantum, the Anthropic
   research assistant, SSH/HPC hosts, PubChem lookups, and the engine
   download).

2. WHAT WE COLLECT, AND WHY

   a. ACCOUNT (optional; required for cloud features)
      - Email address and authentication identifiers, processed by our
        hosting provider Supabase (see Section 5).
      - Records of your license acceptance (policy version and
        time), kept with the account as an audit trail.
      - Why: sign-in, plan entitlement, cloud sync.
      - Legal basis: contract.

   b. CALCULATION CONTRIBUTIONS (FREE PLAN ONLY)
      - What: input structure (atoms, coordinates, charge/multiplicity),
        calculation settings, computed results, engine provenance
        (component versions, timings), and the app version.
      - NOT collected: user-assigned project/molecule names, notes,
        file paths, or any personal identifiers. Contributions are
        severed from your account identity at ingestion and stored
        under a random contribution ID. Because they cannot be traced
        back to you afterwards, they cannot be individually retrieved
        or deleted on request.
      - Why: to evaluate, benchmark, and improve calculation
        methodology and the Software.
      - WE DO NOT use contributions to train machine-learning models,
        sell them, or use them for advertising.
      - Legal basis: consent (the in-app consent dialog); the Free plan
        is conditioned on this contribution.

   c. PREMIUM CLOUD STORAGE (optional)
      - What: the projects and results you choose to sync.
      - Stored scoped to your account (row-level security). With
        client-side encryption enabled, content is encrypted on your
        device before upload and we cannot read it.
      - Why: sync/backup you requested. Legal basis: contract.

   d. LAUNCH-TIME NETWORK REQUESTS
      - Update check and announcement fetch contact our account
        service (Supabase) and our release host (GitHub Pages) at app
        launch; like any web request they expose your IP address
        transiently. No profile is built from this.
      - Legal basis: legitimate interest (keeping the app secure and
        current). [If counsel prefers: make update check opt-in.]

   e. WHAT WE DO NOT COLLECT
      - No advertising identifiers, no behavioral analytics, no crash
        uploads without asking, no scanning of files outside your
        Qemica projects.
      - Third-party API credentials you enter (e.g. an IBM Quantum
        token, an Anthropic API key, SSH keys) are stored in your
        operating system keychain where available (otherwise in an
        encrypted store on your device) and used only to call that service
        directly from your machine; we never receive them. Fees those
        services charge are between you and the provider (see EULA
        Section 9).

3. PAYMENTS
   Premium billing is handled by Paddle (Paddle.com Market Ltd) as
   merchant of record. We receive plan status, not your card details.
   See Paddle's privacy policy.

4. RETENTION
   - Account data: kept while the account exists; deleted within
     30 days of account deletion.
   - Premium cloud data: deleted when you delete it or your account.
   - Free-plan contributions: retained indefinitely in de-identified
     form (see 2b).
   - Local files on your computer are never touched by us.

5. PROCESSORS AND TRANSFERS
   - Supabase (auth, database, storage): [REGION — read it from the
     Supabase dashboard, Settings → General, e.g. ap-northeast-2].
   - Paddle (payments, merchant of record).
   Data may be processed outside your country; we rely on [SCCs /
   adequacy — counsel to complete] for such transfers.

6. YOUR RIGHTS
   Depending on your jurisdiction (GDPR, KR PIPA, etc.) you may have
   rights to access, correct, delete, or export your account data, to
   withdraw consent (which stops future contributions), and to lodge a
   complaint with a supervisory authority. Contact
   feedback.qemica@gmail.com.
   Note the de-identification limit in Section 2b for past
   contributions.

7. SECURITY
   Transport encryption (TLS) for all traffic; access tokens stored in
   your operating system keychain where available (otherwise in an
   encrypted store on your device), never in plain text; optional
   client-side encryption for Premium cloud data; row-level security on
   cloud tables.

8. CHILDREN
   The Software is not directed to children under 14 (under 16 in the
   EU/EEA).

9. CHANGES
   Material changes are presented in the app for re-acceptance before
   taking effect. The version date above identifies the text you
   accepted.
